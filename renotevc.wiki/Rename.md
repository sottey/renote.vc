Rename command takes two arguments(could be passed zero or one argument by the way, it will just ask you for "both" or "new" titles).

`renotevc rename`. <br>

- **Usage**: `renotevc rename [flags]`
- **Aliases**: `rename`, `rn`, `r`
- **Flags**: `-h`, `--help` -> help for rename
